When working under time scales or any under external pressure, my mind looks to ways to achieve as much as possible in as little time. One way I have found to achieve this is time chunking with pomodoro timing. This means that I set goals for myself for each segment of time that I must complete within that time. This first achieves the effect of accountability for the work I complete, and also achieves the impact of gratification for when I achieve these goals, which helps with further motivation. I think to expand the scope of this so far successful technique, I would like to set daily, weekly and monthly goals for myself, and hold myself to account for these.
